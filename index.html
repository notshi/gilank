About

Gilank is a duo of Bradford-based experimental electro-acoustic musicians, Alice Gilmour and shi Blank.

They work with an array of loopers, pedals, synths, MIDI controllers, and acoustic instruments.

One person starts, often with a shared thought in mind, the other adds a layer, and so on.

Each responds to the other's input in real-time, their interactions shaping the evolving composition.
