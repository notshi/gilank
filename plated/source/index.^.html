14.5.2025
We can finally reveal the secret location of the next performance -
Ever wanted to watch the sun set from a cemetery whilst immersed in an ever changing soundscape on all sides?
We are delighted to be closing day one of The Progress playing in the Undercliffe Cemetery with the brilliant santoor player, Eeshar Singh.
From 8 to 10pm, our live improvisation will be relayed through lots of speakers around the location and stay on loop playing overnight! Come and experience it. We'd love to see you there.
23.3.2025
We've got a number of performances coming up in the next few months - one of which is in a secret location that we're really excited about. Do keep an eye out for the venue reveal soon!

GIG 4 GAZA!! We are playing with some extraordinary musicians for a great cause at Wharf Chambers on Wednesday, 26th March. All profits will be going to @fundakitchengaza. Alongside Hegel for Chimps and Jo Christman, it will also be Rushbonds' last ever set. Give as much as u can!
3.3.2025
Really excited to be part of Dr. Shabina Aslam's new play, "Teaspoon of Shampoo", about a fake doctor who treated thousands of people for over 30 years. The Look North crew from BBC1 came over and did a piece on us during one of our rehearsals. Tickets are all sold out but there's a waiting list if you're lucky. See you there!
6.2.2025
Thankful to be included in Augustin Bousfield's Bradford City of Culture 2025 Playlist for BBC Radio 6 Music. Have a listen (we're 24:25 in).
24.1.2025
We spent the day at Nave Studios with Ozzy Moysey and the Leeds Improv Orchestra - new album sometime out this year, perhaps?!
12.12.2024
Perfomance at the celebration of Theatre in the Mill's re-opening.
27.7.2024
Our set at Edible Archives' Drinking St George's Hall event for BD:Festival 2024.
